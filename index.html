The name

Why we call it Heirloom.

An heirloom is something valuable handed down through generations — treasured, timeless, made to outlast trends. That is exactly what the gear we emulate has become: the tube, opto, FET, and VCA circuits engineers have revered for fifty years, passed from studio to studio. We take that inherited magic and put it in your hands.

Inherited tone

Every stage is modeled on real analog behavior — the saturation, the program-dependent timing, the sweet nonlinearities that make hardware feel expensive.

Built to last

One perpetual license. Free updates. No iLok dongle, no monthly bill. VST3, AU, and Standalone on macOS and Windows — buy it once and keep it.

Made simple

Deep tone, shallow menus. Big knobs, honest metering, auto gain-matching, and defaults that already sound like a finished record.

The Collection

Four tools. One signature.

01 — Equalizer

Patina EQ

4-band program EQ · analog drive

A hardware-style program equalizer with a driven output stage built in. Four bands with stepped, musical frequency and gain — no fiddly typing, just confident moves — then push ANALOG to add the harmonic bloom and glue that pristine digital EQs can't.

  • Four bands with detented, stepped frequency & gain; low/high switch shelf ↔ bell, mids switch wide ↔ narrow
  • ANALOG saturation with a continuous Drive — 2× oversampled, auto gain-matched so it never just sounds "louder"
  • Analog-style VU metering (0 VU = −18 dBFS) with peak warning, plus ±12 dB output trim

02 — Compressor

Keepsake Compressor

One knob · four classic characters

The whole point of a keepsake is that it's simple and treasured. One Compression knob sets threshold, ratio, attack, release, and knee together — turn it up for more, that's it. Then pick the flavor of the circuit doing the work, each modeled on the compressor that defined an era.

FET · fast Opto · smooth Tube · warm VCA · precise
  • Four modeled circuits — FET punch (1176-style), Opto smoothness, Tube warmth (vari-mu), VCA control (SSL-style)
  • Mix knob for instant parallel compression; Level Match for an honest, loudness-matched A/B
  • Switchable sidechain HPF (80/120/250 Hz) to keep lows punchy, and a soft-clip Ceiling on the output

03 — Mastering

Keystone Master

A full mastering chain that listens first

Play your loudest section and press LEARN. Keystone normalizes the input so every stage is hit at the optimal level — then a complete mastering chain does the rest. One Intensity knob takes you from gentle glue to loud-and-proud; the whole chain moves with it.

  • LEARN analyzes your track and auto-normalizes input so the chain always hits at the right gain
  • One signal path: fast VCA (API-style) → slow VCA "glue" (SSL-style) → soft clipper → brick-wall limiter
  • True-peak Ceiling presets for streaming (−0.1), standard (−1) and safe (−3 dBTP) delivery, with built-in LUFS metering

04 — Gate

Sentinel

Smart drum gate · frequency-aware

A drum gate that actually understands drums. Pick the source — Kick, Snare, or Tom — and press LEARN; Sentinel locks onto that drum's fundamental and triggers on it, so it opens for the hit you want and ignores the bleed you don't. Then a frequency-dependent gate and overhead de-bleed clean up the ring and spill without choking the tone.

Kick Snare Tom
  • LEARN locks the trigger to the source's fundamental band — tight, false-trigger-resistant detection tuned per drum (Kick / Snare / Tom)
  • Frequency-dependent gating with overhead de-bleed — closes on cymbal spill and ring while keeping the drum's body intact
  • Lookahead so attacks are never clipped, a live threshold visualizer, and adjustable Depth, band Q and separate low/high release
